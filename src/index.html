About www.Larivaarbani.org

Larivaarbani is a digital platform dedicated to the preservation and promotion of Larivaar Gurbani (continuous form).

Our goal is to provide the global Sangat with easy access to the daily Hukamnama Sahib from various Gurdwara Sahibs in both traditional Larivaar and modern Pad-Chhed formats.

By using modern web technology, we aim to make the reading of Gurbani a seamless experience across all devices.
